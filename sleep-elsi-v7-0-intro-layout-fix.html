Sleep Elsi

    

      
    

    

      

        
A gentle sound environment for rest, relaxation and calm moments.

        
An experimental web app exploring layered, slowly moving sounds. This is an early version, but ready to use. Hope it helps you find calm moments too.

      

      

        
How it works

        
Sleep Elsi creates sound scenes by combining simple sound layers that slowly move and change over time.

        
Unlike a normal audio loop, the sound is never completely static. Small variations in tone, texture and movement help create a more natural background.


        
Sound modes

        

          
Vacuum

          
A steady familiar sound inspired by household appliances.

          
Hum

          
A softer, voice-like layer exploring gentle humming textures.

          
Vibro

          
A low-frequency layer designed for speakers that can reproduce deeper sounds. It explores the feeling of sound as vibration.

        


        
Tips

        
Start with a low volume. Let your ears adapt before making changes.

        
The best setting is usually the one you stop noticing.
